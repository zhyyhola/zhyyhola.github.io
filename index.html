Welcome!

I am Yuanyuan Zhang, a fourth-year graduate student at Boston University, specializing in syntax and semantics. My research focuses on the interplay between language structure and meaning.

Research Highlights

  • Degree Semantics in Mandarin Chinese: Analyzing the syntactic and semantic properties of degree expressions in Mandarin.
  • Modality in Central Plains Mandarin: Investigating dynamic, deontic, and epistemic modality through fieldwork and analysis.
