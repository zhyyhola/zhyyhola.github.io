Welcome!

Hello! I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Linguistics at Boston University. My primary research interests lie in syntax and semantics, where I explore the intricate relationship between linguistic structure and meaning. Beyond my core focus, I am also passionate about topics such as language acquisition, natural language processing (NLP), and language documentation. These areas allow me to bridge theoretical linguistics with real-world applications and contribute to the preservation and understanding of linguistic diversity.
